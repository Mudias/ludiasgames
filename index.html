About this game

Release Date: Coming Soon
Platform: PC (Steam)
Developer: LudiasGames on most platforms.
The Sushi Restaurant is a cozy horror game where you prepare sushi and unlock new recipes every day. But something is making the atmosphere grow creepier and you need to figure out what.

Key Features

  • Prepare sushi and unlock new recipes every day.
  • Hire a waitress that works for you.
  • Each day, something is making the atmosphere grow creepier. Can you figure out what?
